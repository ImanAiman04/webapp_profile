Japan is an island nation located in East Asia, known for its rich culture, history, technological advancements, and stunning natural landscapes. It is one of the most developed and influential countries in the world, with a unique blend of tradition and modernity.

Geography

  • Japan is an archipelago consisting of over 6,800 islands, with the five main islands being Honshu, Hokkaido, Kyushu, Shikoku, and Okinawa.
  • It is situated in the Pacific Ocean, to the east of the Korean Peninsula and China. The country is highly mountainous, with the majority of its population living in coastal plains and river valleys.
